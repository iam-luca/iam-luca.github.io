About Me

I was born in 1995 in the city of La Spezia where I began my studies till I reached the High-School.

I have been using computers since I was a child and quickly became a reference for my elementary school teachers who turned to me whenever they could not perform certain tasks. I had a particularly gratifying moment when I was in secondary school and the vice-principal asked me to help the technician connecting together all school's PCs and devices.

After completing the secondary school I chose to attend the technological course. Driven by a great passion for the subject, I learned and practised a lot of topics, so much so that in the last year I was able to effectively support my classmates with their computer homework.

This passion has brought me to study Computer Science at the University of Pisa.

Along with the academic work, I have always spent time on my own projects, from simple to more complex ones, getting a great satisfaction from all of them.
